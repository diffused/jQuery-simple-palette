jQuery simple palette

A quick and simple html color palette generator jquery plugin.

Displays a customizable, limited palette of colors in a simple html grid.

  • Default

    $('#palette').palette();

  • 'Thin' preset

    $('#palette_thin').palette('thin');

  • 'Strip' preset

    $('#palette_strip').palette('strip');

  • Box' preset

    $('#palette_box').palette('box');

  • Custom - single column width, 3 colors

    $('#palette_custom').palette({cols:1, colors:['c8323a', '#405192', '0c9557']});
